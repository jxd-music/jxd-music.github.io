JXD / Jean Born 1979 Frankfurt, Germany Electronic Artist

Artist Profile

JXD is the electronic music project of Jean, born in 1979 in Frankfurt, Germany. Driven by a deep lifelong passion for electronic music, JXD creates sound worlds between vocal-driven Electronica, X-Pop, Hypnotic Techno, atmospheric Techno and cinematic electronic production.

The project combines synthesizers, textured rhythms, intimate vocals and emotional songwriting with a clear focus on atmosphere, melody, tension and depth. Across all directions, the emotional center remains the same: music as an immersive space — reflective, nocturnal, rhythmic and deeply human.

JXD works with Fender Studio Pro, Synthesizer V and custom composition tools as part of an evolving creative workflow.

Visual Art

Paintings and visual works as another part of Jean’s creative language.

Art & Sound

Alongside music production, Jean also creates visual artwork under the JXD identity. The paintings extend the same emotional field as the music: organic forms, inner landscapes, movement, tension and release.
