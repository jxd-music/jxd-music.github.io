About

JXD is an electronic music project focused on emotional depth, cinematic atmosphere and vocal-driven songwriting.

JXD creates electronic music that moves between introspection and wide-screen atmosphere. The sound combines synthesizers, textured rhythms, emotional vocals and cinematic production with a clear focus on mood, melody and narrative.

Rather than treating electronic music only as a club format, JXD explores songs as emotional spaces: dark, luminous, fragile, powerful and deeply human.
